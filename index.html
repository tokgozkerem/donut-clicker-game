      Barn

      Buy and manage animals. Each animal produces goods automatically over time. Use Feed Plans to adjust production speed vs cost:

      • Standard ‚Äî Free, normal speed (1x)
      • Ration ‚Äî Costs 0.5%/cycle, slower (0.7x) but faster breed cooldown
      • Premium ‚Äî Costs 2%/cycle, faster (1.4x) and better trait chance

      Breeding

      Pick two animals of the same type and start a breeding program. Offspring inherit traits from parents with chance of mutation. Three programs:

      • Quick ‚Äî 30 min, 2 trait slots
      • Standard ‚Äî 2 hours, 2 slots + rare chance
      • Long ‚Äî 4 hours, 3 slots + variant chance (unlocks FC 25)

      Contracts

      Accept contracts to deliver goods for donut rewards. Three types:

      • Rush ‚Äî 3-5 min, small reward
      • Standard ‚Äî 8-15 min, medium reward + possible buff
      • Long Haul ‚Äî 45-90 min, big reward + guaranteed buff

      Buffs boost various things (production, offline, costs). Max 2 active contracts.

      Collection

      Sell stored goods for donuts. View active buffs and open crates dropped from selling.
